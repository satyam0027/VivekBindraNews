Vivek Oberoi Joins Dr Vivek Bindra at Gujarat's Biggest Branded Factory Retail Outlet Launch in Vadodara

Ribbon-cutting at Baroda Square — celebrity presence, ₹4 crore+ opening day sales, and retail expansion plans

India's organized retail industry is witnessing rapid growth, and one of the biggest highlights in the business sector this year is the grand launch of Gujarat's biggest branded factory retail outlet in Vadodara. Entrepreneur and business mentor Dr Vivek Bindra attended the mega inauguration alongside actor and entrepreneur Vivek Oberoi, creating huge excitement among retailers, entrepreneurs, influencers, and consumers.

The newly launched Branded Factory outlet officially opened on May 23, 2026, at Baroda Square, BPC Road, Alkapuri, Vadodara, Gujarat. Spread across more than 1 lakh square feet, the outlet features over 1 lakh products under one roof across fashion, electronics, lifestyle, accessories, footwear, and other consumer categories.

According to reports, the company is offering discounts ranging from 50% to 90%, making premium branded products more affordable for Indian consumers. Dr Vivek Bindra Shares Vision for India's Retail Growth

During the launch event, Dr Vivek Bindra spoke about the future of India's organized retail sector and the growing opportunities in value-driven business models. He highlighted how Indian consumers are increasingly looking for affordable pricing along with quality products, creating massive potential for scalable retail businesses.

Dr Vivek Bindra also emphasized the importance of entrepreneurship, business systems, and structured expansion strategies for long-term success. This latest development further strengthens discussions around Vivek Bindra business expansion and Bada Business retail growth.

The presence of Vivek Oberoi added major attention to the Vadodara retail launch event. Several celebrities, influencers, creators, and business personalities also attended the inauguration, making it one of the most talked-about retail events in Gujarat this year.

Branded Factory Records ₹4 Crore+ Sales on Launch Day

One of the biggest highlights of the launch was the overwhelming customer response on the very first day. Reports suggest that Branded Factory recorded sales worth more than ₹4 crore during its opening day operations.

  • Customer reward campaigns

The strong public response reflects the growing demand for organized retail and affordable branded shopping experiences in India.
