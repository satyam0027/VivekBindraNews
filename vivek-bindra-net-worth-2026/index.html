Who Is Vivek Bindra?

Dr Vivek Bindra is the founder and CEO of Bada Business Private Limited, a New Delhi-based company that offers entrepreneurship training, MSME mentorship, leadership development programs, and an investor-mentor ecosystem for Indian startups and small businesses.

He is widely known for his YouTube channel "Dr Vivek Bindra: Motivational Speaker," which has grown into one of the largest business education channels in Asia. His content covers business strategy, sales techniques, leadership psychology, financial literacy, and entrepreneurial motivation — delivered in Hindi, making it accessible to a broad Indian audience.

Vivek Bindra's Career Timeline

Early Life and Education: Vivek Bindra grew up in Delhi and pursued his education in business management. His early interest in leadership and business was shaped by personal challenges and an interest in applying management principles to real-world entrepreneurship.

Corporate Training Phase (Early 2000s–2012): Before becoming a YouTube personality, Vivek Bindra built his career as a corporate trainer. He worked with hundreds of companies across India, delivering workshops and leadership development sessions.

YouTube Launch and Viral Growth (2013–2018): Vivek Bindra launched his YouTube channel around 2013. His videos — explaining business concepts, marketing strategies, and leadership frameworks in simple Hindi — resonated with students, small business owners, and entrepreneurs. By 2018, the channel had crossed 10 million subscribers.

Bada Business Launch (2019–Present): Bada Business Private Limited was formally launched to consolidate Vivek Bindra's training programs, online courses, and mentorship services. The platform offers programs like the Leadership Funnel Program (LFP) and LFP Plus.

Legal Resolution (2023–2025): In late 2023, Vivek Bindra faced personal and legal controversies. By September 2025, the Supreme Court of India dismissed all pending cases against him and Bada Business.

2026 Activities: Vivek Bindra is actively engaged in public appearances, new business launches, and program expansions, including the Branded Factory mega retail launch in Vadodara.
