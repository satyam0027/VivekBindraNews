Who Is Lokhande Industries?

Lokhande Industries, established in 1978, is a well-known manufacturing company based in Palghar, Maharashtra. The company specializes in chain manufacturing products, including:

  • V-ring chains
  • Anchor chains
  • Flat ring chains
  • Key chains

Despite decades of experience in manufacturing, the company reportedly faced challenges related to operational efficiency, production systems, and scalable business growth.

To overcome these limitations, the company partnered with Dr Vivek Bindra-led Bada Business Private Limited.

Why Lokhande Industries Collaborated with Dr Vivek Bindra

According to business reports, the collaboration focused on improving business execution, operational discipline, and performance management systems.

Bada Business helped the company implement the following:

  • KPI-driven management systems
  • Daily accountability structures
  • Team productivity tracking
  • Production efficiency monitoring
  • Operational planning systems
  • Performance optimization frameworks

These business strategies reportedly helped Lokhande Industries improve workforce productivity and operational efficiency without major infrastructure expansion.

Lokhande Industries Records Strong Production Growth

One of the biggest highlights of this business transformation is the sharp increase in per-person production capacity.

Reports suggest that the company’s monthly per-person production improved from around 1534 kg to nearly 2542 kg after implementing structured operational systems.

The company also reportedly achieved:

  • 166% growth in production efficiency
  • 164% increase in per-person profitability
  • 30% growth in PAT margins
  • Better production management
  • Improved business process discipline

These numbers have now positioned Lokhande Industries among the most discussed MSME growth examples associated with Bada Business in 2026.
