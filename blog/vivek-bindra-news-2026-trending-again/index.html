The Sanatan Premier League: A Completely New Chapter

One of the more surprising developments in early 2026 has been Dr. Vivek Bindra's involvement with the Sanatan Premier League (SPL), a cricket league with a vision that goes far beyond sports.

In February 2026, at the prestigious Constitution Club of India in New Delhi, Dr. Bindra joined revered spiritual leader Devkinandan Thakur Ji Maharaj and former Indian cricketer Chetan Sharma for the grand trophy unveiling ceremony. The SPL, organised by the Sanatan Cricket Club, is designed as a platform that blends competitive cricket with cultural values, discipline, and social purpose.

The tournament, held from March 12–15, 2026, in Indore, was broadcast on Sony Ten and Sony TV, giving grassroots cricket talent a genuine national platform. The prize pool was significant: ₹31 lakh for the winning team, ₹15 lakh for the runner-up, a brand-new car for the Man of the Series, and cash rewards for standout performers at every level.

Dr. Bindra's message at the event captured exactly what this initiative stood for: that when young people are given the right platform, one that respects their talent and their character, they don't just become better athletes. They become better human beings.

Why India's Entrepreneurs Trust Him: An Honest Look

Let's step back from the news for a moment and ask a deeper question: why does Dr. Vivek Bindra's community stay so fiercely loyal, even through turbulent times?

It comes down to three things.

Relevance

His content doesn't just motivate, it teaches. Whether he's breaking down why a particular business failed or explaining how a small-town entrepreneur scaled to ₹100 crores, the information is actionable. People walk away with frameworks, not just feelings.

Language

This might sound simple, but it isn't. By choosing Hindi as his primary medium, Dr. Bindra reached an audience that was largely ignored by the English-first coaching industry. Millions of entrepreneurs in Tier-2 and Tier-3 cities, who had never had access to quality business education, suddenly did.

He has shown up on YouTube, in workshops, and at seminars for over a decade. In a world of fleeting influencers, that kind of sustained presence builds a different kind of trust. The trust you give to someone who has been reliably useful to you, not just entertaining.
