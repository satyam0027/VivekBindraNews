Supreme Court Dismisses All Cases Against Vivek Bindra and Bada Business — Full Story

India's highest court gives a clean chit — what it means for Bada Business and its followers

Home / Vivek Bindra News / Supreme Court Clean Chit
By Vivek Bindra News Editorial Team Published: May 28, 2026 Category: Vivek Bindra News Reading time: ~6 min

The Supreme Court of India dismissed all pending cases against motivational speaker and business coach Dr Vivek Bindra and his company Bada Business Private Limited, effectively granting a clean chit in all related legal proceedings. The development closed a chapter of legal controversy that had surrounded one of India's most recognised business educators.

What the Supreme Court Ruled

The top court's dismissal of all cases against Vivek Bindra and Bada Business brought closure to a series of fraud-related complaints that had been filed against the motivational speaker. The ruling confirmed that no actionable legal case remained against Bindra or his primary business entity, Bada Business Private Limited.

This is a significant development for the millions of entrepreneurs, students, and professionals who have followed Vivek Bindra's YouTube channel, attended his programs, or enrolled in Bada Business courses. The clean chit from India's highest court carries considerable legal and reputational weight.

Background: The Controversies and Legal Proceedings

Vivek Bindra, who built a massive following through his YouTube channel focused on entrepreneurship and business education, faced a period of intense scrutiny in late 2023 and 2024. Multiple complaints and legal proceedings were filed in different courts related to allegations involving Bada Business.

During this period, Bindra remained largely silent publicly — a deliberate approach that observers have since noted reflected a strategy of allowing legal proceedings to take their course rather than engaging in media speculation. Simultaneously, Bada Business continued to operate its training programs, mentor businesses, and expand its ecosystem.

Vivek Bindra's Response and Return to Public Activity

Following the Supreme Court's ruling, Dr Vivek Bindra has resumed active public engagement. Recent appearances include the Branded Factory mega outlet launch in Vadodara, the Sanatan Premier League trophy unveiling in New Delhi, and continued business mentorship activities through Bada Business.

His continued public profile and business activity signals that Bada Business is operating normally and that Bindra is focused on the organisation's growth rather than dwelling on past controversies.

What Is Bada Business Private Limited?

Bada Business Private Limited is Dr Vivek Bindra's primary business entity, which manages his entrepreneurship training programs, MSME mentorship services, leadership courses, and increasingly, an investment and incubation ecosystem for Indian entrepreneurs.

The company runs flagship programs including the Leadership Funnel Program (LFP) and the more recent LFP Plus. It also supports investee companies and partners with entrepreneurs across India to help businesses scale.

According to reports, companies that have worked with Bada Business have recorded significant growth — with some reporting revenue increases of over 300 percent following structured business transformation through the platform.

Impact on the Indian Entrepreneurship Community

For India's startup and MSME community, the Supreme Court ruling is significant. Vivek Bindra's programs have reached lakhs of entrepreneurs across India, particularly in smaller cities and towns where access to quality business education and mentorship is limited.

The clean chit removes any lingering uncertainty that prospective learners or business partners may have had about engaging with Bada Business. It reaffirms the company's legal standing and allows it to focus entirely on its mission of supporting India's entrepreneurial ecosystem.

Looking Ahead: Bada Business in 2026

With legal proceedings now fully resolved, Bada Business enters the second half of 2026 with considerable momentum. The company's investee ventures are expanding nationally, its training programs continue to attract new cohorts, and Vivek Bindra remains one of the most-watched business educators in Asia on YouTube.

Entrepreneurs and business owners looking to engage with Bada Business's programs can do so with full clarity about the company's legal standing — a clean chit from the Supreme Court of India is as authoritative a confirmation as possible.
