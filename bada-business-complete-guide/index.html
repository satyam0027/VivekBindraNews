Bada Business is one of India's most prominent entrepreneurship and business education platforms. Founded by Dr Vivek Bindra — one of Asia's most-subscribed business educators on YouTube — Bada Business has grown from a training company into a comprehensive entrepreneurial ecosystem. This guide explains everything you need to know about Bada Business in 2026.

What Is Bada Business?

Bada Business Private Limited is a New Delhi-based company that provides business training, MSME mentorship, leadership development programs, and entrepreneurial growth support to business owners, startup founders, and professionals across India.

The name "Bada Business" roughly translates to "Big Business" in Hindi — reflecting the platform's mission to help Indian entrepreneurs think at a larger scale and build businesses that create jobs, generate revenue, and contribute to India's economic growth.

Who Founded Bada Business?

Dr Vivek Bindra is the founder and CEO of Bada Business Private Limited. He is a business coach, corporate trainer, and motivational speaker who built his reputation through YouTube content, live seminars, and corporate engagements. Vivek Bindra holds the distinction of having one of the largest business education YouTube channels in Asia, with content covering sales, marketing, leadership, finance, and entrepreneurship strategy.

What Does Bada Business Offer?

1. Leadership Funnel Program (LFP and LFP Plus)

The Leadership Funnel Program is Bada Business's flagship live event and training program. It is designed to help entrepreneurs rapidly scale their businesses through structured leadership, sales, and operational frameworks. LFP Plus is the latest iteration — an enhanced, premium version with additional mentorship components and business transformation tools. Read our full LFP Plus review for a detailed breakdown.

2. Online Business Courses

Bada Business offers a library of digital courses covering sales and marketing strategy, financial management for SMEs, HR and team building, digital marketing and e-commerce, and business communication and negotiation. Courses are available on the Bada Business app and website, priced across a range of tiers.

3. MSME Mentorship and Business Consulting

One of Bada Business's most impactful offerings is its structured MSME mentorship — where companies receive direct guidance on implementing business systems, scaling operations, and improving financial performance. Case studies show companies achieving 300–340% revenue growth following Bada Business mentorship. See our documented success stories.

4. The Bada Business Investment Ecosystem

In recent years, Bada Business has evolved beyond training into an investment and incubation ecosystem. Dr Vivek Bindra holds equity stakes in several companies associated with the platform — including Branded Factory, the value-retail venture that opened a massive outlet in Vadodara in May 2026.

5. Entrepreneurs Launchpad

Vivek Bindra has organised large-scale events like the "Entrepreneurs Launchpad" — held at venues like Indira Gandhi Stadium in Delhi — which bring together thousands of entrepreneurs for learning, networking, and inspiration.

Who Is Bada Business For?

  • MSME owners looking to scale beyond their current plateau
  • Startup founders who need structured business frameworks
  • Students and aspiring entrepreneurs who want to understand business before launching
  • Sales and marketing professionals seeking to improve performance
  • Mid-career professionals exploring entrepreneurship

The platform's Hindi-language approach makes it particularly valuable for entrepreneurs in non-metro India, where English-language business content can be a barrier.

Bada Business Programs — What They Cost

Specific pricing varies and is subject to change. Generally: individual online courses range from Rs 999–Rs 9,999; LFP and premium programs are higher-tier investments, often bundled with mentorship; MSME consulting is custom-priced based on engagement scope. Bada Business regularly runs promotional pricing during launches and special events.

How to Join Bada Business

  1. Visit the official Bada Business website or download the Bada Business app
  2. Browse available programs by category (leadership, marketing, operations, etc.)
  3. Register for the program that fits your business stage
  4. Engage with the content, community, and mentors within the platform

Is Bada Business Legitimate?

Bada Business Private Limited is a registered Indian company. Its founder, Dr Vivek Bindra, is one of India's most publicly recognised business educators. As of 2025, the Supreme Court of India dismissed all legal cases against Vivek Bindra and Bada Business, granting a complete clean chit. This provides the strongest possible legal confirmation of the company's legitimacy.

Bada Business in 2026 — Where It Stands

In 2026, Bada Business is operationally active and expanding. Key recent activities include the Branded Factory mega retail launch in Vadodara (May 2026), continued LFP Plus program deliveries, multiple documented business transformation success stories from MSME partners, and Vivek Bindra's active public presence at events and media engagements.
