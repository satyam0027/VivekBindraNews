Pune Real Estate Firm Records 3X Surge in Site Visits After Joining Dr Vivek Bindra's Cash Growth Program

Relation Realtech Pvt Ltd transforms marketing, sales funnel, and channel partners under Bada Business CGP

Home / News / Latest Business News / Relation Realtech
By Vivek Bindra News Editorial Team June 2, 2026 ~7 min read
  • Company Relation Realtech Pvt Ltd
  • Founders Mohaniraj & Madhusudan
  • Location Pune, Maharashtra
  • Programme Bada Business Cash Growth Program (CGP)

New Delhi, June 2026: In one of the most notable real estate business turnarounds of recent months, Pune-based Relation Realtech Pvt Ltd has reported a threefold rise in weekly property site visits after joining the Cash Growth Program (CGP) led by globally acclaimed business coach Dr Vivek Bindra under Bada Business Private Limited.

The development has drawn significant attention across India's SME and real estate ecosystem, adding to the growing list of success stories attributed to Dr Vivek Bindra's structured business coaching approach.

Who Is Relation Realtech?

Founded by Mr. Mohaniraj and Mr. Madhusudan, Relation Realtech Pvt Ltd is a Pune-headquartered mandate sales company operating at the intersection of real estate developers, channel partners, and end homebuyers. With over a decade of operational experience, the company had earned a credible market standing — yet found itself struggling to convert that reputation into scalable, data-driven growth.

Despite consistent marketing investments and an established network, the company's performance metrics told a different story: fragmented lead management, limited ROI visibility, and an underperforming channel partner ecosystem were slowing its momentum in an increasingly digital-first property market.

The Challenge: Traditional Approach Hits a Ceiling

As the Indian real estate sector accelerated its shift towards digital platforms and analytics-led decision-making, Relation Realtech recognised a critical gap between its ambitions and its operational capabilities.

The absence of a structured sales pipeline meant customer journeys were tracked inconsistently. Lead nurturing depended heavily on individual follow-ups rather than systematic processes. The company's channel partner network — often the single most important revenue driver in mandate sales — lacked a formal engagement and incentive framework.

Marketing spends continued to grow, but without measurable attribution, every campaign felt like a gamble. Conversions remained inconsistent, and weekly site visits plateaued.

This is the kind of business scenario that Dr Vivek Bindra's Cash Growth Program was specifically designed to address.

Dr Vivek Bindra's Intervention: What Changed

Recognising the need for an expert-led, system-driven transformation, Relation Realtech enrolled in the a Cash Growth Program at Bada Business Private Limited, one of India's most recognised platforms for business coaching and entrepreneurship training under the mentorship of Dr Vivek Bindra.

The engagement began with a thorough audit of the company's marketing and sales infrastructure — examining budget efficiency, channel performance, lead acquisition quality, and conversion tracking accuracy.

Three core interventions followed:

  1. Digital-First Marketing Overhaul — Traditional, broad-reach marketing was replaced with highly targeted digital campaigns informed by audience segmentation and performance analytics. Every campaign was now tied to measurable outcomes.
  2. End-to-End Sales Funnel Deployment — A structured sales funnel was built from the ground up — mapping every stage from lead generation and inquiry handling through to site visit scheduling and final closure. Accountability was introduced at each stage, giving leadership real-time visibility into business performance.
  3. Channel Partner Activation through Incentive Frameworks — The company introduced a reward-and-recognition model for its channel partners — a mechanism that directly boosted partner motivation, improved lead quality, and drove a sharp rise in property site visits.

The Outcome: Numbers That Speak

The results of the CGP intervention were swift and measurable.

  • 3X increase in weekly property site visits in a short implementation period
  • Higher-quality inbound leads — focus shifted from volume-based to intent-driven acquisition
  • Stronger closure rates driven by aligned marketing and sales teams
  • Improved digital marketing ROI with clear attribution across campaigns
  • Upward revision of overall business growth projections for the coming quarters

Founders Speak: "We Moved from Unorganised to System-Driven"

Sharing their experience with the Cash Growth Program, founders Mohaniraj and Madhusudan stated:

"Partnering with Bada Business under Dr Vivek Bindra's guidance gave us direction, discipline, and a completely new way to look at our business. We moved from unorganised processes to a system where every stage is measurable. The growth in site visits and the quality of engagement we are now seeing has given us the confidence to think much bigger."

Why This Story Matters for India's Business Community

The Relation Realtech case is the latest in a long series of business transformations attributed to Dr Vivek Bindra's coaching methodology at Bada Business. From manufacturing to retail to real estate, the Cash Growth Program has repeatedly demonstrated that Indian SMEs — regardless of sector — can achieve rapid, measurable growth when guided by the right systems and mentorship.

For India's real estate sector specifically, this story underscores an urgent truth: relationship-driven businesses need structured operational frameworks to scale sustainably in a digital economy. Effort and reputation alone are no longer sufficient.
