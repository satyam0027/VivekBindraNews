Vivek Bindra News: Shree Hari Ornaments Achieves 3X Business Growth Through Bada Business Program

Surat B2B jewellery manufacturer transforms cash flow, inventory systems, and geographic reach under Dr Vivek Bindra's Cash Growth Program

Home / News / Vivek Bindra News / Shree Hari Ornaments
By Vivek Bindra News Editorial Team June 5, 2026 ~7 min read
  • Company Shree Hari Ornaments
  • Founder Mr. Kaushik Kathiriya
  • Location Surat, Gujarat
  • Programme Bada Business Cash Growth Program (CGP)

Surat, Gujarat, June 5: In the latest Vivek Bindra News, Surat-based B2B jewellery manufacturer and wholesaler Shree Hari Ornaments has reported extraordinary business expansion after partnering with Dr Vivek Bindra and Bada Business Private Limited — India's most trusted business coaching and consulting platform.

Led by Mr. Kaushik Kathiriya, Shree Hari Ornaments has served the wholesale gold jewellery segment for years, supplying curated collections to jewellers across multiple markets. Despite a strong reputation for design quality and retailer-focused supply, the business was being quietly held back by an approval-based sales model that was draining cash flow and limiting scalability.

Why Shree Hari Ornaments Turned to Dr Vivek Bindra and Bada Business

Under the traditional approval system, retailers would stock jewellery without any upfront purchase commitment — paying the manufacturer only after products were sold. While convenient for retailers, this model created serious financial stress for Shree Hari Ornaments:

  • Unpredictable cash flow and delayed billing cycles
  • Working capital trapped in circulating approval inventory
  • Zero consistent revenue visibility or monthly forecasting
  • Business Growthlimited to a narrow geographic market

Recognising that real growth demands structured business systems, Mr. Kathiriya enrolled in Dr Vivek Bindra's Cash Growth Program (CGP) through Bada Business Private Limited — a decision that would transform the company's future.

The Vivek Bindra Bada Business Strategy That Changed Everything

The most powerful intervention following the collaboration was a full transition from approval-based selling to an inventory ownership model. Instead of stocking goods on consignment, retailers were guided to purchase and hold inventory within their showrooms — creating real ownership, real accountability, and real revenue.

To support retailers through this shift, Shree Hari Ornaments introduced a structured three-month return policy on unsold inventory — reducing retailer risk while dramatically improving supplier cash flow.

Immediate impact included:

  • Billing cycles became faster and far more predictable
  • A significant portion of the jeweller network committed to the ownership model
  • Supply chain accountability improved across all tiers
  • Monthly revenue visibility went from uncertain to consistent

Inventory Optimisation: The Data-Driven Overhaul

A critical part of the Bada Business Cash Growth Program intervention was a thorough inventory audit and stock movement analysis. Slow-moving and dead stock categories were identified and rationalized. Production planning was then aligned with actual fast-moving jewellery designs based on real demand patterns — not assumptions.

This eliminated holding risk, freed up working capital, and significantly improved inventory turnover — giving the business financial breathing room it had never experienced before.

Geographic Expansion: Shree Hari Ornaments Enters Ahmedabad

With stronger operational systems in place — a core outcome of working with Dr Vivek Bindra's team — the company was ready to scale beyond Surat. Shree Hari Ornaments entered the Ahmedabad market with a dedicated sales strategy and local team, successfully tapping into a wider jeweller network and reducing its dependence on a single geography.

The Outcome: Nearly 3X Business Growth

The combined force of these strategic changes — cash flow restructuring, inventory transformation, and market expansion — delivered growth momentum approaching 3X for Shree Hari Ornaments.

The business today operates with:

  • Predictable, recurring monthly revenue
  • Leaner, faster-moving inventory
  • A scalable supply chain model
  • Active market presence across Surat and Ahmedabad

Throughout the engagement, a dedicated team of Bada Business consultants conducted in-depth operational diagnostics and deployed targeted solutions across departments — with a clear focus on building long-term scalability, not just patching short-term problems.

Mr. Kaushik Kathiriya on the Vivek Bindra Collaboration

"The collaboration helped us bring structure into our business operations. From inventory planning to revenue visibility, the systems introduced through Bada Business have created stronger stability and confidence in our growth journey." — Mr. Kaushik Kathiriya, Founder, Shree Hari Ornaments
