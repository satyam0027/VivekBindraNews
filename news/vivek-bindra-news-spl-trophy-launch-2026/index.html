Vivek Bindra News: Dr Vivek Bindra and Devkinandan Thakur Ji Maharaj Launch Sanatan Premier League Trophy

Official SPL trophy unveiling at Constitution Club of India — sports, leadership, and values for India's youth

Home / News / Vivek Bindra News / SPL Trophy Launch
By Vivek Bindra News Editorial Team May 29, 2026 ~5 min read
  • Event SPL Trophy Unveiling
  • Venue Constitution Club of India, New Delhi
  • League Sanatan Premier League (SPL)
  • Notable Guest Chetan Sharma (former Indian cricketer)

New Delhi: In a major development attracting attention from sports enthusiasts and youth across the country, Bada Business Founder Dr. Vivek Bindra joined renowned spiritual leader Devkinandan Thakur Ji Maharaj at the official unveiling of the Sanatan Premier League (SPL) trophy in New Delhi.

The grand ceremony was organized at the Constitution Club of India and witnessed the presence of several distinguished guests, including former Indian cricketer Chetan Sharma. The event highlighted the vision of combining sports, leadership, discipline, and cultural values through a national cricket platform.

Dr Vivek Bindra Shares Inspiring Message for Youth

Speaking during the trophy launch event, Dr. Vivek Bindra encouraged young Indians to dream bigger and work relentlessly toward their goals. He emphasized that talent alone is not enough to achieve success and that discipline, consistency, and focused action are equally important.

According to Dr. Vivek Bindra, India's future will be shaped by a generation that is willing to take responsibility, embrace challenges, and stay committed to long-term growth. He urged young people to adopt a mindset of continuous learning and execution.

The renowned business coach also shared his popular success principles, encouraging youth to remain unstoppable in the pursuit of excellence and to take massive action toward their ambitions.

Devkinandan Thakur Ji Maharaj Highlights Importance of Values

Addressing the gathering, Devkinandan Thakur Ji Maharaj described the Sanatan Premier League as more than a cricket tournament. He said the initiative aims to promote patriotism, discipline, service, and cultural values among the younger generation.

He noted that sports play a vital role in strengthening character, building confidence, and providing positive direction to youth. Platforms like SPL can help aspiring players develop both professionally and personally.

SPL Tournament to Provide National-Level Exposure

The Sanatan Premier League is expected to feature talented players from different parts of India. Organizers believe the tournament will help identify emerging cricket talent while creating opportunities for wider recognition through national broadcasts.

The competition is designed to encourage participation from aspiring cricketers and provide a platform where skill, dedication, and sportsmanship are celebrated.

Attractive Rewards Announced for Participants

Organizers have announced several rewards and recognition opportunities for outstanding performers during the tournament. Special awards will be presented to top-performing players, while teams will compete for prestigious honors and significant prize money.

The initiative aims to motivate young cricketers and encourage greater participation in competitive sports.

Social Impact at the Core of the Initiative

Beyond cricket, the Sanatan Premier League has also been positioned as a socially driven initiative. According to organizers, the platform seeks to contribute toward meaningful social causes and community-focused activities.

The league aims to create a positive impact by combining sports, youth development, and social responsibility under a single vision.
