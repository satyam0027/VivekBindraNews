# Vivek Bindra Inaugurates Branded Factory Mega Outlet in Vadodara — May 2026

Dr Vivek Bindra, founder and CEO of Bada Business Private Limited, was present at the grand launch of Branded Factory's mega retail outlet in Vadodara, Gujarat on May 23, 2026. The event marked one of the most significant retail expansions connected to the Bada Business ecosystem this year.

## What Happened at the Vadodara Launch

The inauguration ceremony took place at Baroda Square, BPC Road, Alkapuri, Vadodara — a high-footfall commercial zone in one of Gujarat's most prominent business cities. Actor and entrepreneur Vivek Oberoi also attended the event alongside Dr Vivek Bindra, making it a high-profile launch covered by multiple media outlets.

Branded Factory, the value-retail and B2B platform associated with the Bada Business growth ecosystem, describes the outlet as a "mega" format store spanning over one lakh square feet. The outlet stocks over one lakh products spanning fashion, lifestyle, accessories, electronics, and consumer goods — positioning itself as one of Gujarat's largest value-driven retail destinations under one roof.

Dr Vivek Bindra is associated with Branded Factory as a shareholder, making this expansion a direct part of the broader Bada Business investment and mentorship ecosystem.

## What Is Branded Factory?

Branded Factory is a rapidly expanding value-retail and B2B platform that operates within the Bada Business entrepreneurial growth ecosystem. The company focuses on offering branded products at accessible price points, targeting both retail consumers and business-to-business buyers across India.

The Vadodara launch is part of an aggressive expansion strategy that sees Branded Factory scaling its physical retail footprint across major Indian cities in 2026. For entrepreneurs associated with Bada Business, brands like Branded Factory represent a tangible example of how the ecosystem supports ventures from mentorship to market entry.

## Vivek Bindra's Role in the Bada Business Ecosystem

Dr Vivek Bindra is widely recognised as one of India's most-watched business educators and motivational speakers. His Bada Business platform — which offers entrepreneurship training, MSME mentorship, and business growth programs — has grown into a broader investment and incubation ecosystem.

As a shareholder in Branded Factory, Vivek Bindra's involvement signals the platform's strategy to back ventures that solve real retail and distribution challenges for Indian entrepreneurs and consumers alike.

The Vadodara event drew attention not just for its scale but because it demonstrated the practical, on-ground business activity that the Bada Business ecosystem is increasingly associated with — moving beyond online training into physical business ventures.

## Gujarat's Business Environment and Retail Opportunity

Gujarat remains one of India's most business-friendly states, with Vadodara emerging as a key retail and industrial hub. The city's growing middle class and strong SME culture make it an ideal location for a large-format value-retail outlet like Branded Factory.

The launch is expected to create local employment and supply chain opportunities for vendors and entrepreneurs in the Baroda region. The 1 lakh+ product range also positions the outlet as a one-stop destination for household, lifestyle, and business procurement.
