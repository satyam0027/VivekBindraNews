# Bada Business Success Stories 2026: MSMEs That Grew with Vivek Bindra's Mentorship

Behind Bada Business's scale and visibility are the individual stories of entrepreneurs who engaged with the platform and transformed their companies. In 2026, several notable success stories have emerged, demonstrating tangible business growth following structured Bada Business mentorship. Here are some of the most compelling examples.

## Akupaad Marketing — 340% Revenue Growth in Solar Energy

Akupaad Marketing Pvt. Ltd. (AMPL), a Raebareli-based solar EPC company founded by Sachin Srivastva, achieved a 340% revenue growth following its strategic partnership with Dr Vivek Bindra and Bada Business Private Limited.

The company, which provides solar installation solutions for residential and commercial customers, expanded its execution capacity from 100 to 500 monthly solar installations — a fivefold increase. This operational growth was accompanied by improvements in financial structure and a broader geographic footprint across Uttar Pradesh.

For AMPL, the Bada Business mentorship provided the structured business frameworks needed to move from an owner-operated micro-enterprise to a systematised operation capable of handling large-volume contracts.

## Petro Industech — 300% Growth Target in Manufacturing

Petro Industech Pvt. Ltd., a manufacturing company, has targeted 300% growth following its partnership with Bada Business. The company operates in the manufacturing sector and is implementing structured operational and sales systems through the Bada Business framework.

This partnership demonstrates that Bada Business's business transformation approach is sector-agnostic — it has been applied effectively in solar energy, manufacturing, retail, and services, among others.

## Why These Stories Matter for Indian Entrepreneurs

India has over 63 million MSMEs — collectively employing more than 110 million people and contributing approximately 30% of the country's GDP. Yet most of these businesses grow slowly, struggle with systems, and remain dependent on the founder's daily involvement.

What Bada Business success stories demonstrate is that structured mentorship and business frameworks — applied consistently — can accelerate growth dramatically. The key ingredients in these success stories are:

1. **Clear diagnosis** of the business's current constraints
2. **Structured implementation** of sales, operations, and financial systems
3. **Accountability** through the Bada Business mentorship relationship
4. **Mindset shift** from reactive management to proactive business building

## Common Themes Across Bada Business Success Stories

Across the documented success cases from Bada Business, several patterns emerge:

- Businesses that implement sales systems see the fastest early revenue growth

- The discipline of regular accountability check-ins drives execution consistency
